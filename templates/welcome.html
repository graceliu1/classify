A new way to learn

There are approximately 4.4 million children from ages 3-17 diagnosed with anxiety in the United States. The millions of school-aged children who experience anxiety often list school as one of the main causes. How can we reduce the burden schoolwork places on students?

Everyone knows the age old scenario: there are 3 basic requirements for a high school student's success --> good grades, a social life, and enough sleep. However, you can only pick two. With Classify, we're working to change that by creating a dynamic scheduling service designed for and by students.

Students can easily enter their daily information into a form. Then, Classify's web application will generate a dynamic schedule for them - complete with timing, breaks, and auto-pauses for students to relax and stretch. We've incorporated timing functions in so students can track the time they've spent on each activity.





Welcome to Classify

Classify has two components: a web application and a database login. Students can input assignments and information in a form on our web application, which has already been launched! Upon starting a new "study session," Classify will use scientifically proven methods of studying to help students learn more effectively! We've also incorporated a database login system with tasks, which you can test out by checking out our Github repository.
